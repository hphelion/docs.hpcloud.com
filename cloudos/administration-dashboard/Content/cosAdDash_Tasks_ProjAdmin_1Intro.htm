Project Administrator Tasks

Concepts:

After your organization works through Getting Started, project administrators can focus on their day-to-day tasks - monitoring and maintaining project resources and content. Project administrators have all the rights of project users, plus rights to modify or delete project resources and content.

As a best practice, project administrators maintain projects, with a focus on the areas you see below.
